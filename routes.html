Travel Guidelines

Ensure you have your tickets and identification ready for inspection at all times. Adhere to local COVID-19 guidelines and safety protocols.

For international travel, check the visa requirements and travel advisories for the destination country.

Additional Travel Guidelines

  • Luggage: Keep your luggage secured and within your sight. Use luggage racks and make sure your belongings are clearly labeled with your name and contact information.
  • Punctuality: Arrive at the station at least 15 minutes before your departure time. Train schedules are precise, and itâ€™s important to be on time.
  • Comfort: Wear comfortable clothing and carry a small pillow or neck support for long journeys. Bringing snacks and water can make your trip more pleasant.
  • Noise Levels: Be considerate of other passengers. Keep noise levels down, especially during night travel, and use headphones if listening to music or watching videos.
  • Safety: Stay aware of your surroundings and report any suspicious activity to train staff. Familiarize yourself with the emergency procedures outlined on the train.
  • Health: Move around the train periodically to stretch and improve circulation, especially on long trips. Carry hand sanitizer and maintain hygiene.

Travel Tips

  • Book tickets in advance to secure the best prices.
  • Travel during off-peak hours to avoid crowds.
  • Keep a digital copy of your ticket and identification for backup.

No Outside Food Allowed

For your convenience and safety, outside food is not permitted during train travel. We provide complimentary meals and snacks to all passengers. Our menu includes a variety of options to cater to different dietary needs and preferences. Please enjoy the food provided and refrain from bringing outside food on board.

No Smoking Allowed

Smoking is strictly prohibited on the train for the health and safety of all passengers. This includes the use of electronic cigarettes and vaping devices. Please respect this policy to ensure a comfortable and pleasant journey for everyone. We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a smoke-free environment.

Berlin to Leipzig

Berlin, known for its rich history and vibrant culture, connects easily with Leipzig, a city famed for its musical heritage and beautiful architecture. Travel swiftly between these two iconic cities and enjoy the diverse experiences each has to offer.

Frankfurt to Cologne

Travel from Frankfurt, the financial hub of Germany, to Cologne, home to the stunning Cologne Cathedral and a lively cultural scene. This quick journey connects two of Germany's most dynamic cities.

Munich to Nuremberg

Journey from Munich, known for its beer gardens and rich history, to Nuremberg, a city with a vibrant old town and significant World War II history. A short trip that offers a glimpse into Bavaria's past and present.

Hamburg to Bremen

Experience the maritime charm of Hamburg before heading to Bremen, known for its historic architecture and the famous Bremen Town Musicians. This quick and scenic route is perfect for a day trip.

Stuttgart to Munich

From the automotive hub of Stuttgart, travel to Munich, a city known for its vibrant cultural scene, beer halls, and annual Oktoberfest. Enjoy a comfortable and scenic trip between these two major cities.
